About

Spacious and grandly, the restaurant, combining the two richest cuisine in the world - Ukrainian and Georgian BORSHCHAPURI - is located in the Mariinsky Park, next to the Water Museum. The name is familiar to everyone, BORSCH is the most famous hot dish in the whole world, and also - one of the business cards of Ukraine, and KHACHAPURI is a real pride of Georgia. Borshchapuri - institution, where Georgian culture intertwined with modern Ukrainian, traditional Georgian cuisine with modern trends, and all well-known Georgian and Ukrainian hospitality permeated into a single whole! For each direction of the menu are specially invited masters from different regions of Ukraine and Georgia. The brazier menu is managed by a master from Kakheti, from where, incidentally, the legendary Georgian shashlyk comes from. The last one is of 7 kinds, and it is cooked on open fire. Another pride of the restaurant is 4 types of kebab lub: with lamb, turkey, pork, as well as with beef and suluguni. Khachapuri will be prepared for you by a master from Adjara, the best pupil of Gia Agirba - the most famous chef from Georgia, who also participated in the development of the menu. A cook from Svaneti is responsible for kubdari (a traditional dish of this region), chvishtari (corn cake from suluguni) and Svan salt. And the master from Imeretia will cook the kharcho. From the specialties here it is worth trying a chicken of tobacco with Adjika sauce and a branded platter of kebabs, quail and vegetables for a large company. Well, where without juicy khinkali, who run the risk of becoming the best in the city! We cook borscht according to an ancient secret recipe, which is passed on from generation to generation, the descendant of which works in our restaurant! Salo, from Transcarpathian regions and Carpathian tea , all this will not leave you indifferent. Here you will find a huge collection of exclusively Georgian wines, as well as own wine. We invite you to the BORSHCHAPURI restaurant in Kiev, which is perfect for celebrating your birthday.

We have a wonderful location (only 2 minutes from the city of Olympiyskaya). In a two-story building with panoramic windows you can choose a hall that will be as comfortable as possible for you and all the guests. Are you planning a little celebration in the nearest circle? Then the ideal option will be our small banquet hall, where you can accommodate up to 35 guests. Want to have a great party? We will offer you our main hall, which accommodates up to 100 people. If your plans include lunch or dinner with a few friends, we are happy to arrange a table for you, according to the number of guests in the main hall or on the summer terraces.

One of the main criteria for choosing a restaurant to celebrate a birthday in Kiev is the menu. We will ensure that you and your guests get genuine gastronomic pleasure. At your disposal - the main and banquet menu, where you can choose the most popular dishes of different cuisines of the world. The cost of a banquet - from 750 UAH per person.

Any holiday is not only a delicious meal, but also entertainment. To make the event brilliant and fun, we offer services for the animator or the host. And on the summer terraces you can arrange creative photoshows: to display the brightest shots of the birthday.

It is no secret that teambuildings and corporate events unite the team, promote team spirit, improve work results, motivate employees to achieve new professional heights. To organize a corporation, companies are looking for a facility that would be as comfortable as possible for all employees. The BORSHCHAPURI restaurant often hosts corporate events on a variety of occasions: the New Year, the birthday of a company, a victory in a tender, an informal gathering, a presentation, a conference, a seminar. Our rooms are ready to accept teams up to 100 people.

Depending on the theme of the event, the number of guests, the wishes of the company, we offer you a large and small hall, summer terraces. Beautiful interiors with lots of greenery (green - the most popular corporate color), panoramic windows overlooking the Olympia Stadium, excellent service, a varied menu and an individual approach to each company make our establishment one of the best restaurants in Kiev for corporate events.

For corporate events, we offer an ordinary and banquet menu, which is represented by various dishes of popular cuisines of the world. The cost is from 750 UAH per person. Of course, the event may be non-alcoholic - we have enough drinks for such a format: morsa, freesia, compotes of own production. If the company decided to add alcohol to the menu, then we are ready to offer a fine wine list with the best drinks from Georgia.
